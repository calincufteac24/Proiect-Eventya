How to Get Your Mobile App - Step by Step

You can have your own mobile app in 3 simple steps
1. Request a Trial for your Mobile App

Access the Create Your App page and fill out the form. Kindly input your organization's website address, contact, information and your mobile app's desired feature

Once you've submitted the form, you'll be contacted within 3 business days by our development team to start your mobile app.

2. App Management Training

At any time your 90 days trial, you can request an app management training with one of our consultants.

The training will be conducted over a call by our dedicated app administrator

You will learn how to set up your app and how to start adding any type of content and how to keep your audience engaged.

3. google Play and iTunesc Submission

Once you've tested out the mobile app and you've ready to purchase, you'll have to move to a paid account. This is where we'll submit your app.

We'll need around 3-5 business days to prepare your app for submission. We;ll haveto wait around 7-15 business days for iTunes and 1-3 days for Google Play approval.
